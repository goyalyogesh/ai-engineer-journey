## Universal rules

1. **Personalize the first line.** "Saw you posted about X" or "Loved your work on Y." Generic = ignored.
2. **Lead w/ value, not ask.** Share a project link, insight, or compliment first.
3. **One ask, clearly stated.** Don't bury it in 3 paragraphs.
4. **Make it easy to say yes.** Suggest specific times. Offer to come to them.
5. **No more than 4-5 sentences.** Mobile readers scroll.
6. **No corporate language.** "I'd love to connect to discuss synergies" = instant delete.
7. **Follow up ONCE after 7 days.** Then move on.
8. **Track every send** in `tracker/interviews/job-tracker.md` outreach log.

## Response rate targets

- Analyst interview ask: 25% reply rate (5/20 = good)
- Founder cold DM: 5-10% reply rate
- Referral ask: 60-80% (warm)
- Warm intro: 90%+

If your rate is way below: rewrite the template. The first 2 sentences carry 80% of the response signal.
