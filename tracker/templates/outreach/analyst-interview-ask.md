# Analyst Interview Ask

**Channel:** LinkedIn DM
**Goal:** 15-min user research call
**Target reply rate:** 25%

---

## Template (LinkedIn DM)

Hi [First Name],

I'm a software engineer building AI tools for finance/fintech. I noticed you've been [doing X / posting about Y / working on Z] — would value 15 min of your time to ask 3 questions about your workflow.

I'm trying to understand what's actually painful day-to-day for analysts (vs what tech founders assume). No pitch — pure learning.

Free for a quick call this week? Tuesday or Thursday after 4 PM ET works for me, but happy to fit your schedule.

Thanks!
[Your name]

---

## Variations

### If they're senior (10+ years)
Replace "value 15 min of your time" with "deeply value 15 min of your perspective"

### If they're at a target company (e.g., Hebbia, Bloomberg)
Add P.S. "Big fan of [their company]'s work on [specific project]"

### If they recently published
Open with: "Saw your [post / paper / talk] on X — really resonated with me."

---

## Follow-up (7 days later, ONE follow-up only)

Hi [Name], floating this back up in case it got buried. No worries if timing isn't right — just thought I'd check.

[Then drop]

---

## What NOT to do

- ❌ "I'm passionate about disrupting finance with AI..." (instant delete)
- ❌ "Could we hop on a call to discuss synergies..." (corporate-speak)
- ❌ "I'm building [product] and want your feedback..." (sounds like sales)
- ❌ Long bio about yourself
- ❌ Pitch deck attached
- ❌ Asking for intros / referrals (different ask, separate later)

## What to ASK on the call (15 min agenda)

1. **(2 min) Intro** — who you are, why this call (curiosity, not pitch)
2. **(8 min) Their workflow** —
   - "Walk me through your typical day"
   - "What docs/data do you read most?"
   - "What's the most frustrating part?"
3. **(3 min) Pain & tools** —
   - "What tools do you use today? What's missing?"
   - "If you had a magic button, what would you automate?"
4. **(2 min) Wrap** — thank them, ask if you can follow up in 2 months, ask for 1 referral if it went well
