# Referral Ask (1st-degree connections)

**Channel:** LinkedIn DM, WhatsApp, email
**Goal:** Get a warm referral into target company
**Target reply rate:** 60-80%

---

## Template (1st-degree connection at target company)

Hi [First Name],

Hope you're doing well at [Company]! I'm in the middle of an AI Engineer transition — over the last 6 months, shipped 5 production fintech AI projects ([github.com/yourhandle]).

I'm targeting [Company] and saw [specific role link]. Would you be open to a referral? Happy to send a 1-paragraph blurb you can paste into the referral system, plus my resume — minimal lift on your end.

If timing isn't right or referral isn't your style, totally fine — would love to hear how things are at [Company] either way.

Thanks!
[Your name]

---

## Variations

### When you haven't talked in years
Open: "Long time no chat — saw you're now at [Company], congrats! Reaching out because..."

### When you have something to offer back
Add: "Happy to return the favor anytime — I've been deep in AI engineering and have a network of founders/recruiters in fintech I can connect you with."

### When the connection is weak (acquaintance, not friend)
Be more honest: "We don't know each other super well, so totally fine if this isn't your style. But thought I'd ask given..."

---

## What to include in your "blurb" for them to paste

```
[Your Name] is an AI Engineer who recently shipped 5 production fintech AI projects in 6 months — including a SEC 10-K analyzer, an LLM agent for equity research, and a B2C newsletter SaaS with paying customers.

Background: 8+ years backend (.NET → Python AI). Strong in RAG, agents, evals, production deployment. Based in Canada, open to remote (US/global) or relocation to Hyderabad.

Portfolio: github.com/goyalyogesh/ai-engineer-journey
LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/yourhandle
Resume: [PDF link]

I think they'd be a strong fit for [Role] given [reason — e.g., "their fintech experience and Python/LangChain stack alignment"].
```

This makes the referrer's job 30 seconds, not 30 minutes.

---

## After the referral

- Thank them within 24 hrs
- Update them on every interview round (they want to know)
- If you get the offer: thank again + offer something back (intro, gift)
- If you don't: thank again + ask for feedback (rare gold)

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Ask for referral without specific role
- ❌ Ask 5 different people at same company (looks desperate)
- ❌ Forget to thank
- ❌ Forget to update them on outcome
- ❌ Send paragraph-long resume in DM (use the blurb template above)
- ❌ Expect referral from someone you only met once briefly
