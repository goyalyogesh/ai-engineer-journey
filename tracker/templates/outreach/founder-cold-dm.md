# Founder Cold DM (for Jobs)

**Channel:** LinkedIn DM or X DM
**Goal:** Get a 30-min call → job offer
**Target reply rate:** 5-10% (lower = expected)

---

## Template (LinkedIn / X DM)

Hi [First Name],

I'm an AI Engineer who's been building production fintech AI for the last 6 months — 5 shipped projects including [Project 1 description, e.g., a SEC 10-K analyzer used by [N] analysts]. Live work: [github.com/yourhandle]

[Their company] caught my eye because [specific reason — recent funding, your product solves X, you posted about Y]. I'd love to chat about how I could help your team build [specific thing they need based on their job posts / blog posts].

Background: 8+ years backend (.NET → Python AI), based in Canada, open to remote or relocation. Available [timeframe].

Would 30 min next week work? I'll come prepared with 2-3 ideas specific to [their company].

[Your name]

---

## Variations

### When they have an open AI Engineer role posted
Replace "open to remote" with "saw your AI Engineer posting and applied through the formal channel — this is a parallel reach because [why I think I'm a strong fit]."

### When they don't have a posted role
Open: "Not sure if you're hiring AI engineers right now, but wanted to put myself on your radar."

### When you have a mutual connection
Open: "[Name] at [Company] suggested I reach out because [reason]."

### For YC founders specifically
Add: "Big fan of YC alumni — also applying to YC W26 myself for my own B2C side project (linked above). Happy to share lessons either way."

---

## Follow-up (7 days, ONE only)

Hey [Name], floating this back up. If timing isn't right or AI hires aren't priority, no worries — would love to stay in touch as you grow. Project link [github.com/yourhandle/repo] in case interesting.

---

## What NOT to do

- ❌ "I'm passionate about your mission" (everyone says this)
- ❌ Generic copy-paste w/o their name or company specifics
- ❌ Long resume in DM body
- ❌ Multiple asks ("happy to chat OR interview OR send resume OR...")
- ❌ Pitching at unicorn execs (they get 100 DMs/day) — start at seed/Series A founders
- ❌ Sending the same DM to 50 founders in 1 day (LinkedIn flags this)
- ❌ Asking for referrals to other companies (different ask)

## Targets to prioritize

**Highest reply rate:**
- YC W25/W24 batch founders (still scrappy, need help)
- Seed-stage AI fintech CTOs (technical, will respect technical work)
- Series A founders who recently posted "we're hiring AI engineers"

**Lower reply rate (try anyway):**
- Series B+ founders (busy, but bigger comp)
- VPs at large fintechs (slower, more bureaucratic)

**Skip:**
- VC-only LinkedIn profiles (won't hire, will gatekeep)
- Founder w/ 100K+ followers (DMs go unread)

## Volume strategy

- Daily: 2-3 cold DMs max (quality over quantity)
- Weekly: 15-20 DMs (Mon, Tue, Wed; rest of week for replies)
- Monthly: 60-80 DMs total
- **Expected outcome:** 5-10% reply = 6-8 conversations/month = 1-3 strong opportunities
