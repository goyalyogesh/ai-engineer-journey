## Sections (typical structure)

### 1. Why this matters (200 words)
- The problem
- Why current solutions fail
- What you'll learn

### 2. The setup (300 words)
- Context: what you were building
- Constraints: budget, time, skill
- Goals: what success looks like

### 3. What I tried (400-600 words)
- Approach 1 — what + why + what failed
- Approach 2 — what changed
- Approach 3 — what worked

### 4. The solution (500-800 words)
- Architecture overview (with diagram)
- Key code snippets (3-5 max)
- Specific tradeoffs

### 5. Results (200 words)
- Numbers (cost, latency, accuracy, users)
- Before/after comparison
- Honest about what didn't work

### 6. Lessons learned (200 words)
- 3-5 takeaways
- What you'd do differently

### 7. CTA (100 words)
- Link to GitHub repo
- Link to live demo
- Subscribe to next post / follow on LinkedIn
