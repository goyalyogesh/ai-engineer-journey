## Question bank

**Workflow questions:**
- Walk me through your typical day
- What documents/data do you read most?
- What takes the most time?
- What do you wish was automated?

**Pain questions:**
- What's the most frustrating part of your job?
- Where do mistakes/errors happen most?
- What tasks do you delay or avoid?

**AI/tools questions:**
- What tools do you use today?
- Have you tried AI tools? Which ones? Why did/didn't they stick?
- If you had a magic button to automate one thing, what?

**Buying questions:**
- Who decides what tools your team buys?
- What's your monthly tool budget?
- What's the biggest blocker to adopting a new tool?
